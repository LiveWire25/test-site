Voxel Visionaries

CLASS : IX-XII
TEAMS PER SCHOOL : 1
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 2
EVENT HEAD : Asmit Kumar Sahoo
CONTACT DETAILS : iamsolama.
THEME : Tree House
SOFTWARE : Blender
MODE : Online
OVERVIEW : Design a treehouse that blends creativity, adventure, and nature in this Blender competition. Whether simple or elaborate, participants will craft a unique retreat that embodies imagination, architectural beauty, and a sense of escape.
GENERAL GUIDELINES :
1) All submissions to be made online by 25th November , 2025.
2) All 3D models must not contain any imported stock files.
3) All Submissions should contain .blend flies along with texture files and related files.
4) All files should be compressed to zip file.
5) Zip folders to be renamed with school names of the participant.
      Format- schoolname_eventname.
6) Zip folders to be uploaded to G drive link and to be submitted on our website.
7) Top 3 teams will be announced on 30th April 2025.
8) Top 3 ranks will be disclosed during the offline event on 3rd May, 2025.
If you have any questions, feel free to contact the event heads or reach out to us on Discord!
