Aenigma Syntax

CLASS : IX-XII
TEAMS PER SCHOOL : 1
NUMBER OF PARTICIPANTS: 2
EVENT HEAD : Soham Sharma
DISCORD ID: myst0_0
MODE : Online 

OVERVIEW : Aenigma Syntax is an interactive online cryptic hunt where players are given questions, hints, and clues. To solve them, participants must scour the web, decode ciphers, and interpret pop culture references to uncover answers.
GENERAL GUIDELINES :
The cryptic hunt will have 6 levels.
Each level will have 2-3 sublevels.
The competition will be time based.
Teams are allowed to ask for a total of 5 leads each for a level. Each lead counts to 15 minutes added to the timer.
The link to the 1st level will be floated in the Discord channel.
The timer will start as soon as the participants are directed to the site.
Collaborating with other competitors is strictly prohibited.
The team that finishes the hunt in the least amount of time or clears the most levels wins.
If you have any questions, feel free to contact the event heads or reach out to us on Discord!
